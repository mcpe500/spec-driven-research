# SPEC — contoh-topik

## Decision / Goal
Menentukan apakah klaim X layak dipercaya untuk keputusan Y.

## Audience
Internal: peneliti/PM/analyst

## Scope
### In-scope
- Indonesia
- 2022–2026
- Sumber primer + sekunder

### Out-of-scope
- Negara lain
- Data sebelum 2022

## Research Questions (RQ)
- RQ1: Apa klaim utama yang harus dibuktikan?
- RQ2: Evidence apa yang mendukung/menolak klaim itu?
- RQ3: Seberapa confident keputusan bisa diambil?

## Deliverables
- REPORT.md
- EVIDENCE.csv
- CLAIMS.yml

## Definition of Done (Quality Gates)
- Each claim has >= 2 evidence rows
- Any numeric/technical claim has >= 1 primary source
- REPORT references claim_id per paragraph
- Counterevidence included
- Known unknowns + next searches listed
